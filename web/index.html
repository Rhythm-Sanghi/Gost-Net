[ FAQ ]

Q: Does Ghost Net work without internet?

Yes! Ghost Net only requires a local WiFi network. No internet connection needed.

Q: Is it really private?

Yes. All messages are encrypted and stored only on your device. No servers, no cloud, no tracking.

Q: Can I use it on iPhone?

Not yet. Currently Android-only (5.0+). iOS support is on the roadmap.

Q: Is it open source?

Yes! Ghost Net is fully open source. Check the code on GitHub.

Q: What's Panic Mode?

Emergency feature that instantly deletes all messages, files, keys, and settings. Irreversible.
